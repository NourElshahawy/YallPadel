Mansoura Open 2026: 32 Teams, One Champion

calendar_month June 28, 2026 location_on Padel Arena Mansoura schedule 4 min read

Registration is officially open for the Mansoura Open 2026, the city's biggest amateur padel tournament. Thirty-two teams will compete across two days at Padel Arena Mansoura, with matches running from early morning through floodlit evening rounds.

The tournament follows a group-stage format, with four teams per group playing round robin before the top two advance to a single-elimination knockout bracket. Matches are best-of-three sets, with a 7-point tiebreak deciding any set tied at 6-6.

Schedule

Group stage matches run all day Saturday, June 27, across six courts. Quarter-finals and semi-finals take place Sunday morning, with the final scheduled for 7 PM under lights — open to spectators free of charge.

"This is the tournament Mansoura's padel community has been asking for — a real competitive bracket, not just a friendly weekend. We expect it to sell out within days."

— Tournament Director, Padel Arena Mansoura

How to register

Teams of two can register directly through Padel Arena Mansoura's front desk or by reserving a tournament slot on YallPadel. Entry fee covers both tournament days, a player kit, and access to the courtside cafeteria throughout the event.
