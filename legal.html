2 What YallPadel Is

YallPadel is an online marketplace that allows:

YallPadel is not a padel club and does not own or operate any of the listed courts. We act solely as an intermediary between players and court owners. The actual court service is provided by the court owner, not by YallPadel.

4 Bookings & Payments

4.1 Making a Booking

When you select a court and time slot and complete payment, a booking is confirmed. You will receive a confirmation notification via email or SMS. Bookings are subject to availability at the moment of payment; YallPadel does not guarantee availability until payment is fully processed.

4.2 Pricing

All prices displayed are in Egyptian Pounds (EGP) and include applicable taxes unless stated otherwise. Court owners set their own prices; YallPadel displays these prices as provided and is not responsible for pricing errors made by court owners.

4.3 Payment Processing

Payments are processed through secure third-party payment gateways. By paying, you authorize YallPadel to charge the total amount shown at checkout. YallPadel does not store full card details on its servers.

4.4 Service Fee

YallPadel may charge a small service fee on each booking to maintain and improve the Platform. This fee, if applicable, will be clearly shown before you confirm payment.

5 Cancellations & Refunds

policy

Cancellation policies are set individually by each court owner. The applicable policy is displayed on the court's page before you book.

  • Flexible: Full refund if cancelled at least 6 hours before the slot start time.
  • Moderate: Full refund if cancelled at least 24 hours before the slot start time.
  • Strict: No refund after booking is confirmed.

Refunds, where applicable, are returned to the original payment method within 5–7 business days. YallPadel's service fee is non-refundable in all cases.

If a court is unavailable due to reasons on the court owner's side (unexpected closure, maintenance, etc.), YallPadel will issue a full refund including the service fee.

3 Sharing Your Data

We share personal data only in the following circumstances:

  • With court owners: Your name and phone number are shared with the court owner when you complete a booking at their venue, so they can verify your reservation.
  • With payment processors: Payment details are transmitted to our payment gateway partner in encrypted form.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some Platform features — including staying logged in — may not work correctly without them.

Under applicable Egyptian data protection principles, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data via your account settings or by contacting us.
  • Delete your account and associated data, subject to our retention obligations.

  • Export your booking history in a portable format upon request.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@yallpadel.eg. We will respond within 14 days.

If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymise your personal data within 30 days, except where legal retention is required.
